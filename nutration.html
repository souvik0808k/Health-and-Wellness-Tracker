Milk

Milk is a rich source of calcium, protein, and essential vitamins like vitamin D and B12. Consuming milk can support bone health, muscle growth, and overall well-being, making it a valuable addition to a balanced diet.

Oatmeal

Oatmeal is a nutritious breakfast choice, rich in fiber, protein, and essential nutrients like iron and magnesium, offering sustained energy and promoting heart health.

Green salads

Green salads are nutrient-dense dishes comprising various leafy greens, vegetables, and often fruits or nuts. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, promoting hydration, digestion, and overall health when incorporated into a balanced diet.

Eggs

Eggs are nutritious, providing high-quality proteins, vitamins, and minerals essential for muscle growth, brain function, and overall health.

Sprouts

Sprouts are nutritional powerhouses, rich in enzymes, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Consuming sprouts regularly can support digestion, improve nutrient absorption, and promote overall health and vitality.

Fruits

Fruits are nature's nutrient-packed treats, offering a wide array of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants essential for maintaining good health. Incorporating a variety of fruits into your diet can boost immunity, support digestion, and contribute to overall well-being.

Dry fruits

Dry fruits are nutrient-rich snacks loaded with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, promoting heart health, digestion, and overall well-being.

Water

Water is essential for sustaining life, aiding in digestion, circulation, and temperature regulation. It also helps flush toxins from the body, promotes healthy skin, and supports overall bodily functions.
