Push-Ups

The push-up helps to build muscle and improve strength throughout the upper body. It targets the muscles in your chest (pectoralis major), arms (particularly the triceps) and shoulders (especially the scapular stabilising muscles).

Sit-ups

Sit-ups strengthen the core, improve posture, and enhance balance. They reduce the risk of back pain and injury while increasing flexibility. Variations include traditional situps, stability ball situps, V-sits, and elbow-to-knee situps.

Planks

Planks strengthen the core, improve posture, and enhance overall stability. They also reduce the risk of back pain and injury, and can be varied for increased challenge.

Jumping-jacks

Jumping jacks are a dynamic cardio exercise that engage multiple muscle groups, improve cardiovascular health, and increase overall endurance. They are also effective for burning calories and can be easily incorporated into various workout routines.

Running

Running is a high-impact cardiovascular exercise that improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, burns calories, and boosts mood through the release of endorphins

Inchworms

Inchworms are a full-body exercise that strengthen the core, arms, shoulders, and legs. They improve flexibility, mobility, and coordination while also engaging the cardiovascular system.

Squats

Squats are a compound exercise that targets multiple muscle groups including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core. They help improve lower body strength, enhance mobility, and can be adapted for various fitness levels and goals.

Crunches

Crunches are effective for strengthening the abdominal muscles, leading to better core stability and posture.
